- Gradients: it helps us to have the transition linear and radial Gradient.
             linear Gradient will have default flow of top to bottom.
             radial gradient will have the circle inside the box.

Syntax:
background-image: linear-gradient(direction/angle, starting color...ending color);

Example:
 <style>
        .main{
            height: 200px;
            background-image: linear-gradient(to right, red, yellow, orange);
        }
    </style>
